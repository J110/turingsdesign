AI Mentor

A coach that learns you.

An AI companion that observes your behavior patterns, learns your triggers, and adapts its guidance in real time. Every user gets a mentor shaped by their own data — something no static content library could ever deliver.

Mood-Adaptive Content

Stories that read the room.

AI reads a child's energy level at bedtime and generates a story calibrated to bring them down — pacing, vocabulary, tone, and narrative arc all adjust on the fly. A different story every night, tuned to that exact moment.

Health as Investment

Your wellness has compound interest.

AI models each user's wellness trajectory like a financial portfolio. Sessions compound. Consistency pays returns. The app predicts where you'll be in 90 days and adjusts difficulty to keep you in your growth zone.

Voice-First Social Mechanics

Your voice is the game.

AI scores your pitch in real-time singing battles, synthesizes full musical accompaniment on the fly, and moderates live debate rooms. The AI turns voice itself into a game mechanic — something impossible with pre-recorded tracks and static formats.

Personalized Sleep Protocols

A bedtime ritual that designs itself.

AI tracks which story themes, narrator tones, and pacing lead to faster sleep for a specific child. Over weeks, it assembles a personalized protocol — and keeps refining it as the child grows. No parent could build this manually.

Behavioral Nudges Without Screens

Gamification you can step on.

AI analyzes household behavior patterns to determine where to place visual cues and how to design them. The output is a physical mat — no app, no screen — but the strategy behind it is AI-generated.

Our Thesis

What we believe.

The Problem Is the Boss

Not the market. Not the investor. We build based on whether a genuine human problem exists. If the solution works, the money follows.

Outcomes Over Engagement

We measure behavioral change, not session time. If engagement goes up but outcomes don't — we've failed.

Build, Prove, Partner

We build products, prove they work, then find aligned partners to scale them. Some we scale ourselves. Both are success.

AI Is Core, Not Cosmetic

AI is in every layer — diagnosing behavior, generating content, modulating voice, adapting in real-time. It also makes us fast: 14 days to ship. Human design thinking. AI execution.

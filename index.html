Who We Are

We start with the human.

Most products solve real problems but fail at engagement. Wellness apps that track everything but nobody opens. Sleep tools with great content that children ignore after day one. Enterprise platforms that work perfectly — for the 30% of employees who actually use them.

The standard fix is generic gamification — points, badges, leaderboards bolted onto the surface. It treats engagement as a feature instead of a design principle. We do something different. We redesign products so the game mechanics and the intended behavioral change are the same thing. For a sleep app, the story itself puts the child to sleep. For a wellness tracker, the game drives the healthy habit. The fun is not separate from the purpose. The fun IS the purpose.

We build our own products to prove this works. Then we find the right partners to take them further. We are not an agency. We are not a startup optimizing for an exit. We are a place where genuine human problems get solved with behavioral design, game mechanics, and AI — and the solutions are made available to anyone aligned enough to scale them.

How We Think

From problem to proof.

Every product starts with a behavioral insight. AI accelerates the build. The result is a product that works — for the human, not just for the dashboard.

01

Understand the Human

Before any code, before any mechanics — who is the person? What are they trying to do? Where does their behavior break down? We sit with the problem until we understand it well enough to design for it.

02

Design the Loop

Game mechanics that make the right behavior the easy choice. Not points for the sake of points. Mechanics that serve the outcome — sleep, connection, health, learning.

03

Build Fast, Stay Honest

Agentic AI lets us ship in 14 days. But speed is not the point. Honesty is. Does this work? Does the behavior change? If not, we rebuild. The product earns its existence.

04

Prove It Works

We measure behavioral outcomes, not vanity metrics. If children sleep better, it works. If users form real habits, it works. If engagement goes up but outcomes don't — it doesn't work.

Our Thesis

What we believe.

The Problem Is the Boss

Not the market. Not the investor. Not the trend. We choose what to build based on whether a genuine human problem exists and whether we can solve it better than what's out there. If the problem is real and the solution works, the money follows.

Outcomes Over Engagement

We measure behavioral change, not session time. If children sleep better, it works. If users form real habits, it works. If engagement goes up but the behavior doesn't change — we've failed, no matter what the analytics say.

Build, Prove, Partner

We build products. We prove they work with real users. Then we find the right partners — people aligned with our values — to take them further. Some products we'll scale ourselves. Some we'll hand to others who can do it better. Both are success.

AI as Accelerant

AI makes us fast — 14 days from insight to shipped product. But the design thinking is human. AI doesn't decide what to build or who to build it for. It builds what we design, at the speed the problem demands.

The Founder

Founder, Turings Design

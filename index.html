Who We Are

Gamification for real change.

Turings Design is a product studio that uses gamification and behavioral design to build products that drive real change — for people, habits, and society.

We build our own products. We also collaborate with teams whose products need what we do best: the mechanics that make people come back, form habits, and actually change behavior. This isn't about points and badges. It's about understanding the loops that drive human action — and engineering them into products with purpose.

Operating from Singapore, India, and the United States, we work at the intersection of game design, behavioral science, and AI.

Behavioral Architecture

We design the loops, triggers, and reward systems that make products habit-forming — grounded in behavioral science, not guesswork.

How We Think

From insight to shipped product.

Every product starts with a behavioral insight. AI accelerates the build. The result is a product that moves people.

01

Find the Behavioral Lever

Every product has a moment where behavior either sticks or doesn't. We find that moment, understand the psychology behind it, and design around it.

02

Design the Loop

We prototype the core engagement loop — the trigger, the action, the reward, the investment. Every screen serves the behavioral outcome, not just the feature list.

03

Build Fast, Ship Real

AI-accelerated engineering lets us move from prototype to production at startup speed. No bloated timelines. No committee approvals. We ship.

Our Thesis

Good behavior, by design.

Game mechanics are the most underused tool in product design. We use them to make healthy choices feel natural, build lasting habits, and create products people return to — because they genuinely want to.

Outcomes Over Engagement

We don't optimize for time-on-screen. We measure whether the user actually changed — formed a habit, built a skill, made a better choice.

Own the Product

We build our own products first. That's how we prove our methods work — not through pitch decks, but through shipped products with real users.

True Collaboration

We partner with teams who need gamification and behavioral design woven into their product's DNA. We bring the vision and the tech. You bring the domain.

AI as Accelerant

AI is how we build fast, not what we sell. It lets a small team ship products at a pace that used to require armies — but the vision is always human.

The Founder

Anmol Mohan

Founder & CEO, Turings Design

Anmol founded Turings Design on a contrarian bet: the mechanics that make games addictive are too powerful to waste on engagement traps. Applied to the right problems — wellness, education, communication, daily habits — they can drive behavioral change at a scale that traditional products can't touch.

With a background spanning an acquihire (Tuple Technologies → M17/17LIVE), product design across multiple verticals, and deep interests in behavioral science and systems thinking, Anmol leads a global team across Singapore, India, and the United States — shipping products that prove the thesis.
